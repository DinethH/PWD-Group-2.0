Six Sigma Certification

Recent studies have shown that many industries around the world are integrating Lean Six Sigma philosophy in to their processes. Lean Six Sigma methods are based upon continuous improvement, quality control, and root cause analysis which are skills that most people can use in their organizations. Because of this, upper management is becoming more and more aware of the significant importance it provides. This has led to a growing need for certified personal. Six Sigma certification have different levels of training. Starting with Six Sigma Yellow Belt, which includes an introduction to Six Sigma and its basic practices, to Green and Black Belt which includes a superior knowledge in analytics.

Programs Offered

  • Certified Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CLSSYB)
  • Certified Lean Six Sigma Green Belt (CLSSGB)
  • Certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt (CLSSBB)
